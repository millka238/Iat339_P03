Imagery / Art Direction

The visual direction uses bold, high-contrast imagery with strong colors and dynamic compositions. Photography focuses on movement and energy to reflect a youthful and expressive brand identity.

Brand Component Relationships

The brand’s visual identity is built around the combination of three distinct styles expressed through color and visual tone. Green is used as a reference grunge influences, emphasizing raw textures, individuality, and a slightly rebellious edge. Dark red tones, represent the classic side of the brand, providing structure, elegance, and timelessness. Pastel blue introduces a softer streetwear element, adding a modern, relaxed, and approachable feel. Together, these colors are balanced with clean typography and imagery focused on layered outfits and urban settings, reinforcing the idea that grunge, classic, and streetwear styles can coexist and be mixed naturally within one wardrobe. Also, Clean, modern Univers font typography providesstructure and readability.
