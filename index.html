Basic Branding Ideas

What the company offers:

The company offers a curated collection that blends three of today's most popular styles: Grunge, Classic, and Streetwear, ensuring there's something for everyone in the 20-30 crowd. Whether you're drawn to edgy distressed pieces, timeless wardrobe staples, or bold urban looks, our versatile selection makes it easy to find exactly what speaks to you. Best of all, our pieces are designed to mix and match across styles, so you can create your own unique combinations and express yourself authentically, whether that means pairing a classic blazer with street-style sneakers or adding grunge accessories to a minimalist outfit.

Company personality:

Edgy yet approachable, creative and inclusive, with a passion for self-expression and versatile style that breaks boundaries.

Why customers choose this company:

Customers appreciate the affordable pricing, curated selection that stays on-trend, and the convenience of finding multiple styles in one place without compromising on quality.

Target Customer (Quick Persona):

The target customer is a fashion-conscious individual aged 20–30 who values self-expression and versatility in their wardrobe. They are urban-oriented, socially aware, and interested in blending different aesthetics rather than committing to a single style. This customer enjoys experimenting with fashion, mixing classic pieces with grunge or streetwear elements to reflect their evolving identity.

Brand Components

Fonts

  • Headings: Aclonica Regular
  • Body text: Abhaya Libre Medium

Colour Palette

  • Primary: #423535
  • Accent: #520505
  • Background: #dbdbdb

Imagery / Art Direction

The visual direction uses bold, high-contrast imagery with strong colors and dynamic compositions. Photography focuses on movement and energy to reflect a youthful and expressive brand identity.

Brand Component Relationships

The brand’s visual identity is built around the combination of three distinct styles expressed through color and visual tone. Green is used as a reference grunge influences, emphasizing raw textures, individuality, and a slightly rebellious edge. Dark red tones, represent the classic side of the brand, providing structure, elegance, and timelessness. Pastel blue introduces a softer streetwear element, adding a modern, relaxed, and approachable feel. Together, these colors are balanced with clean typography and imagery focused on layered outfits and urban settings, reinforcing the idea that grunge, classic, and streetwear styles can coexist and be mixed naturally within one wardrobe. Also, Clean, modern Univers font typography providesstructure and readability.
